Nest Electronics and Software Technologies Ltd. Privacy Policy




1. Introduction


At Nest Electronics and Software Technologies Ltd. ("Nest"), we highly value the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy explains how Nest collects, uses, shares, and protects information.

2. Information Collected


At Nest, we collect the following personal information during your use of our services:

  • Full Name: Requested when you take advantage of our services.
  • Phone Number: Required for communication and support purposes.
  • Email Address: Used for updates, information and marketing communications.
  • Uploaded Files: Stored to personalize and improve our services.

3. Use of Information


Your collected personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and personalize our services for you.
  • Support: To respond to your questions and issues.
  • Improvements: To enhance our services and add new features.
  • Communication: To send updates, offers, and other marketing communications.

4. Sharing of Information


Your personal information may be shared with third parties in the following cases and where permitted by law:

  • Legal Requests: In response to a legal request or court order.
  • Service Providers: With our subcontractors and business partners as part of our services.
  • Emergencies: To protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Information Security


Nest takes appropriate technical and administrative measures to secure the personal information collected. However, we understand that no internet-based system is ever 100% secure and therefore cannot guarantee absolute security.


6. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies


Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and to understand how our services are used.

7. Policy Changes


We may occasionally make changes to our Privacy Policy to improve and update it. These changes will be effective when published on our website. Users are advised to regularly review our policy.
